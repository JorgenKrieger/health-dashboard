# Health Dashboard

A personal health dashboard for Apple Health data exported via [HealthAutoExport](https://www.healthyapps.dev/apps/health-auto-export/). Drop in a CSV and get instant charts and coaching insights across weight, body composition, cardiovascular health, activity, nutrition, and walking quality.

**All processing happens in your browser — no data leaves your device.**

---

## Getting started

1. Export your data from the HealthAutoExport app as a CSV file
2. Open the dashboard and enter your goal weight and target date
3. Drop your CSV file onto the upload area

That's it.

---

## Features

- **Weight Loss Goal** — progress tracking, trend projection, and transition planning
- **Body Composition** — weight, lean mass, fat mass, and BMI over time
- **Cardiovascular Health** — resting heart rate, heart rate range, and blood pressure with reference bands
- **Activity** — steps, active energy, distance, and flights climbed
- **Nutrition** — dietary energy and macros (carbs, protein, fat)
- **Walking Quality** — speed, step length, asymmetry, and double support percentage
- Coaching insights for each section based on your trends
- Light and dark theme
- Settings saved locally — nothing stored on a server

---

## Privacy

This tool is entirely client-side. Your CSV is read in memory, never uploaded, and discarded when you close the tab. Only your goal weight, target date, and theme preference are saved — in your browser's `localStorage`.
